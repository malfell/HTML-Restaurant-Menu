Origin Story

Flagship Location: The Underworld

In 1990, Glenn Burke, a struggling college student, sold his soul to pass his "Introduction to Philosophy: Ethics & Politics" course. Shortly after, he was taken to the Underworld, and there, he discovered his love for cooking. After perfecting several secret recipes and becoming an important chef to the ruler of the Underworld, he opened H-Thai-ML. His restaurant's popularity only escalated, and eventually, he was granted a permit to open new locations for his restaurant in the human world. Come visit us! We have restaurants of various temperatures.
